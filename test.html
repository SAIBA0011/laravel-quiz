We are delighted to announce that The South African Institute of Professional Accountants (SAIPA) has officially endorsed this year’s Practice Management Conference for Accountants by coming on board as a supporting partner of the event. SAIPA members who are owners of or partners in small to medium-sized practices are encouraged to attend and benefit from the wealth of experience and knowledge which will be shared by over 30 speakers during three days focusing on:

  • Standards, Legislation & Economic Trends

  • Technology & New Services

  • Developing your firm’s practice management strategy

A SAIPA representative will also be speaking at the event. Faith Ngwenya, Technical Executive, will be reviewing the regulatory landscape for accounting in South Africa. Faith will examine the key regulations impacting accounting practitioners and any potential future changes in policy which you need to prepare for.
